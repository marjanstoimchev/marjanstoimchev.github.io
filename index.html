Projects

Hands-on experience in leading and collaborating on impactful machine learning projects.

AI for Cancer Research

Developed and deployed self-supervised models (DinoV2, MAEs) for multiplex immunofluorescence whole slide image analysis. The project included embedding space analysis, neighborhood detection, and image retrieval, culminating in an end-to-end model for clinical applications.

Self-Supervised Learning DinoV2 Medical Imaging PyTorch Cancer Research

Atomic-Resolution Microscopy

Led the development of a deep learning solution to correct drift distortions in atomically resolved STEM images. This method significantly reduced image acquisition time and cost and was adopted as a standard process in the lab's imaging operations.

Deep Learning Image Reconstruction Material Science OpenCV PyTorch

Hierarchical Remote Sensing with GNNs

Engineered a novel approach using Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) for hierarchical multi-label classification of large-scale remote sensing images, improving the granularity and accuracy of land use analysis.

Graph Neural Networks PyTorch Geometric Remote Sensing Hierarchical Classification

Brain Tumor Radiogenomic Classification

Contributed to a project focused on classifying brain tumors from radiogenomic data. This involved processing complex medical imaging datasets and applying machine learning models to predict tumor characteristics.
