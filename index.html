Marjan Stoimchev

PhD Researcher • Machine Learning Engineer

PhD Researcher in Artificial Intelligence

I am a PhD researcher at the Department of Knowledge Technologies, Jožef Stefan Institute, Slovenia , where I develop novel, domain-agnostic methods in supervised, semi-supervised, and self-supervised learning. My aim is to reduce dependence on labeled data while creating models that are both robust and generalizable across different application areas.

My research covers diverse domains such as remote sensing, oncology imaging, and digital pathology, demonstrating the transferability and scalability of the methods I design. I have extensive experience in building end-to-end deep learning pipelines, from data preprocessing and augmentation to large-scale multi-GPU training, optimization, and evaluation.

Although much of my work is rooted in computer vision, I am highly adaptable and eager to apply my expertise to new AI challenges, always prioritizing robustness, scalability, and real-world applicability.
